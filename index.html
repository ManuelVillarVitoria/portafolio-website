About me

Technical Architect that due to the crisis that has occurred in the sector of construction has been recycled into the IT sector of Web Development; of which I am glad I did, as it is an area that I am passionate about.


I am doing a Full Stack Web Bootcamp in afterwork hours at KEEPCODING to improve my skills acquired in a self-taught plan and I also have a Master in Digital Marketing from EAE Business School


It motivates me to develop my skills and grow professionally. I am confident in my ability to contribute and join a development team since I consider myself a organized, responsible, proactive, motivated, self-taught, disciplined, decisive person who seeks excellence in their work. Also as I have worked for years in the commercial sector I can also stand out for having excellent commercial and communication skills, with an always customer-oriented mindset.


I currently work in APPTO as a personal project, developing and maintaining the Frontend of an APP in production called Compartiendo el Pan, which reads biblical texts stored in an API. For the development of the APP I collaborate with 2 developers, a Senior Backend and a one sofware architect as Team Leader.

I have also worked 3 months in SF Abogados, maintaining their websites and developing new landings and improving your positioning using tools for SEO (Semrush, Google Analytics and Search Console).


During my free time I like to dedicate myself to training myself by learning new programming languages, practicing my sports favorites (fronton, swimming, hicking, fitness, running and whatever arises), reading and traveling when there is a chance.
